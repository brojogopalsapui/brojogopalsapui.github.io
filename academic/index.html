About me

A clean academic profile

I work at the intersection of AI security, hardware security, edge intelligence, and trustworthy physical AI. My research focuses on how real implementations shape the security of modern intelligent systems — from models and accelerators to edge deployment, side-channel exposure, fault injection, and emerging device-aware design.
